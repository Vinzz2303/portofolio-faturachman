# Ask Ting AI - User Quick Start Guide

## What is Ask Ting AI?

Ask Ting AI is your **personal portfolio financial copilot**. It reads your portfolio, understands your risk, and helps you think through decisions.

✅ **What it does:**
- Reads your actual portfolio (holdings, P/L, concentration)
- Explains portfolio risk in plain language
- Suggests actions: Monitor, Wait, Rebalance, or Reduce Exposure
- Responds in Indonesian or English

❌ **What it does NOT do:**
- Give buy/sell signals ("buy now!", "sell!")
- Predict exact prices
- Use dramatic language
- Ask for more information

---

## How to Use

### 1. Open Ask Ting AI

Navigate to the dashboard and find the **Ask Ting AI** section (usually labeled "Tanya Ting AI" in Indonesian).

### 2. Ask a Question

Ask anything about:
- Your portfolio risk
- Current market conditions and how they affect you
- Whether to enter now or wait
- Whether you should rebalance
- Whether your portfolio is concentrated

**Good questions:**

📌 "Portofolio saya aman gak?" (Is my portfolio safe?)
📌 "Apakah saya harus rebalance?" (Should I rebalance?)
📌 "Apa risiko terbesar dalam portofolio saya?" (What's my biggest risk?)
📌 "Bagaimana kondisi pasar sekarang?" (What's the market like right now?)
📌 "Kalau saya masuk sekarang, apa risikonya?" (If I add positions now, what's the risk?)

**Not good questions:**

❌ "Berapa harga AAPL nanti?" (What will AAPL price be later?)
❌ "Saya harus beli atau jual?" (Should I buy or sell?)
❌ "Apakah pasti untung?" (Will I definitely profit?)

### 3. Read the Response

Ask Ting AI responds as a **clean card** with:

```
┌────────────────────────────────┐
│ MAIN INSIGHT (1-2 sentences)   │
│                                │
│ WHY IT MATTERS                 │
│ • Point 1                      │
│ • Point 2                      │
│                                │
│ RISK NOTE                      │
│ One sentence about main risk   │
│                                │
│ ┌─────────────────┐           │
│ │    SUGGESTED    │           │
│ │   ACTION BADGE  │           │
│ └─────────────────┘           │
└────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 4. Consider the Suggested Action

Ask Ting AI suggests **one of four actions**:

| Action | Means | Example |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Monitor** | Keep watching, things look okay | "Your portfolio is diversified, just watch market changes" |
| **Wait** | Don't act right now | "Market is volatile, better to wait for clarity" |
| **Rebalance** | Reduce concentration, diversify | "Your largest holding is 70% of portfolio, rebalance to reduce risk" |
| **Reduce Exposure** | Scale back your positions | "Your portfolio is too exposed to a single sector" |

### 5. Make Your Own Decision

Remember: **The decision is yours.** Ask Ting AI helps you understand the context and risk. You decide what to do.

## Language Support

### Indonesian

Ask Ting AI automatically detects if you write in Indonesian and responds **100% in Indonesian** with natural phrasing:

- "portofoliomu" instead of "portfolio mu"
- "berpotensi" instead of "pasti"
- "yang bisa dipertimbangkan" instead of "yang harus"

### English

If you ask in English, Ask Ting AI responds in clear, professional English.

## Quick Tips

**Tip 1: Be Specific**
- Instead of: "Apa yang harus saya lakukan?" (What should I do?)
- Try: "Portofolio saya terlalu terkonsentrasi? (Is my portfolio too concentrated?)

**Tip 2: Ask About Context**
- "Bagaimana dampak market defensif ke portofoliomu?" (How does defensive market affect my portfolio?)

**Tip 3: Ask About Actions**
- "Apakah ini waktu yang bagus untuk rebalance?" (Is this a good time to rebalance?)

**Tip 4: Follow the Suggestions**
- "Monitor" → Check your portfolio regularly  
- "Wait" → Don't rush to make changes  
- "Rebalance" → Adjust your positions to reduce concentration  
- "Reduce Exposure" → Step back from risk  

**Tip 5: Context Matters**
- Ask Ting AI knows your portfolio, so it gives personal advice
- It's not generic - it reads YOUR holdings and YOUR risk level

## What Ask Ting AI Sees About You

When you ask a question, Ask Ting AI knows:

✅ Your total capital  
✅ Your current portfolio value  
✅ Your profit/loss amount  
✅ Your profit/loss percentage  
✅ All your holdings and their sizes  
✅ Your concentration level  
✅ What language you prefer  

❌ It does NOT know:
- Your personal financial goals
- Your risk tolerance  
- When you need the money  
- Your other investments outside this portfolio  
- Your income or expenses  

So while Ask Ting AI is personal, combine it with your own knowledge of these factors.
